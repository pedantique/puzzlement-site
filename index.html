Why Puzzlement?

🧩

90+ daily puzzles

Wordle, Connections, Strands, Queens, Pinpoint, crosswords, sudoku and dozens more—every daily habit in one place.

📅

One daily checklist

See what’s left today, when each puzzle resets in your timezone, and tick them off as you go.

🔥

Streaks that matter

A streak for every game, plus your overall Puzzlement streak—and you can import the streaks you’ve already earned.

✅

Scores, captured

Results are detected automatically when you finish—no copying, no spreadsheets, no honour system.

📊

Charts & history

Guess distributions, calendar heatmaps and trends for every game. Jump back to any past edition.

🔔

Widgets & reminders

Home-screen widgets show today’s progress; a daily reminder keeps the streak alive. Sync across devices with iCloud.

All your favourite publishers

Puzzles load live from each publisher’s own website—just like in a browser, with your logins remembered.
